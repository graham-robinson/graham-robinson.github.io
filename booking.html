What to expect

Initial consultation

A 30 minute online session. We talk about what brings you to therapy, I explain how I work, and you can ask any questions. There is no pressure to continue — it is a chance to see if we are a good fit.

Ongoing sessions

Sessions are 50 minutes and typically weekly, though this can be adjusted. You will receive a pre-consultation form before your first session covering some background information and key agreements.

Online sessions

All sessions take place via a secure video platform. You will receive a link in your booking confirmation. You are responsible for having a private and quiet space for the duration of the session.

Ending therapy

You can end therapy at any time. It can be helpful to discuss ending in a session so we can close the work thoughtfully, but there is no obligation to give notice beyond what feels right for you.
