Before you reach out

This form is for general enquiries only. I aim to respond within two working days.

If you are ready to book, you can go straight to the booking page — no need to contact me first.

I am not able to respond to messages outside of working hours, and this form is not monitored continuously. Please do not use it for urgent matters.

If you are in crisis or urgent distress, please do not use this form. Contact your local emergency services, GP, or a crisis helpline.

See the crisis support page for resources in Ireland, the UK, and internationally.

Your message will be sent to my secure email inbox. I will not add you to any mailing list or share your details with third parties. See the privacy policy for more information.
