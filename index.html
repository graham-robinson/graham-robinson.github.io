About me

Calm, grounded, and direct when it matters.

My name is Graham Robinson. I work online with adults across Ireland, the UK, and EU. Clients often come to me feeling stuck, carrying anxiety, low mood, or patterns in relationships they can't quite shift.

I work in a calm, grounded way, but I'll challenge you when it's useful. I'm not here to be impressive or to make myself necessary. I'm here to help you need me less.

Before training as a therapist I studied Applied Psychology at University College Cork. That background gave me a lasting respect for research and evidence, and a healthy scepticism about claims that don't hold up to scrutiny.

I'm in regular clinical supervision and committed to ongoing professional development.

How I work

Theory in the background, you in the foreground.

My approach is integrative, drawing on attachment theory, CBT, and person-centred practice. But I try not to let the theory get in the way. If there is something practical that can help, we work on that first.

Attachment-informed

Early experiences shape how we relate to ourselves and others. Understanding those patterns is often the beginning of changing them.

Practically focused

Not every difficulty needs to be traced to its roots. Sometimes a shift in thinking or behaviour is what actually helps — and we start there.

Genuinely collaborative

The relationship between us matters. So does your own sense of what you need. We work together, and I'll be honest with you throughout.

What I work with

Difficulties I see most often.

Anxiety & worry

Persistent anxiety, overthinking, and the physical and emotional toll of living on high alert.

Low mood & depression

Feeling flat, disconnected, or unable to find motivation or meaning in everyday life.

Relationship patterns

Repeating difficulties in relationships, communication struggles, and attachment-related challenges.

Identity & self-worth

Questions about who you are, chronic self-criticism, and difficulty valuing yourself.

Life transitions

Significant changes — in work, relationships, location, or life stage — that leave you feeling unmoored.

Stress & burnout

When the demands of life consistently outrun your capacity to cope with them.

I work with adults on a one-to-one basis, online. I don't claim to specialise in everything. If something you're dealing with falls outside what I can offer well, I'll say so and help you find someone better placed to help.
